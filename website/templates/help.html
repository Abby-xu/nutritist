FAQ's

How does WeighToGo work?

Based on your dietary restrictions, sex, goals, weight and height, you follow a calorie target so you can achieve your weight loss or gain goals. For WeighToGo to work for you, you will need to log your meals and exercise in our Tracking page. We will then calculate how many calories you've consumed and burned and show you how many you have left everyday. If you follow those calorie targets, you should acheive your nutrition goals.

Is Nutrist free?

Yes. We are completely free. We just want to help you reach your nutrition goals.

Can I change my dietary restrictions later?

Yes, all you have to do is head over to the profile page, and uncheck the restrictions you no longer have or check the restrictions you now do have and click save.

How do I add exercise to the Tracking page?

Since, we are not currently integrated with any exercise tracking apps, you will have to manually enter the amount of calories you have burned in the calories remaining card at the top of the tracking page by selecting the input box at the bottom of the card. Enter the amount of calories you burned during exercise, hit enter and see your calories remaining update above.

How do I search recipe recommendations on the Recommendation page?

Start by thinking of dish you want to make, or a key ingredient, select a cuisine type from the first scroll menu and depending on whether you want breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack or mid-day tea, select a meal type from the second scroll menu. Hit submit and see all the recipes we recommend to you. You will be able to navigate to the full recipe by clicking on the name of the dish. You will also be able to see a list of the ingredients so you can decide whether the dish is feasible for you without having to navigate away from our website.

How to use Track your Food

To use our food tracker, enter the foods you eat into the breakfast, lunch, dinner and snack cards in the left top corner, and see the foods get added to the table at the bottom and the total calories per meal update in each of the cards as well as at the very top in calories remaining card.

How to Analyze your Food

To use our food analyzer, read the graphs to see your average macronutrients (cards, proteins, fats) over various amounts of time by selecting your preferred option: daily, weekly, monthly, bi-yearly, yearly etc. and see your progress. Various graph types are displayed so you can analyze the your data the way your prefer.

Check out our diet anaylst here.

How to Use Recipe Recommendations

To use our recipe recommender, just select a card that contains the recipe your interested in. The recipes are already filtered according to your dietary restrictions that were entered when you first registered with WeighToGo, so don't worry about that. THe recipe card will display the link to the recipe, showing you all the ingredients and how to make it.
