What is Nutrition Tracker?

Here at Nutrition Tracker, we have realized that there is a very serious lack of knowledge when it comes to food nutrition and diet within our society. Part of the reason this problem exists is due to paywalls on many nutrition sites as well as an overwhelming difficulty in finding the right foods to eat.


Our solution to this problem comes in three parts: a food intake tracker, a diet analysis tool , and a recipe recommender.


Features that are very often hidden behind a paywall, our food intake tracker and diet analysis tool allow our users to monitor their diets as well as take note of the many little details that come along with the previous task.


The feature we are most excited to share with our users, though, is our recipe recommender. This tool takes into account the foods you like, the foods you can or cannot eat, as well as the past foods you have eaten in order to provide you recipe ideas. With our recipe recommendations, you will no longer have to stress about figuring out what to eat!
